What this form captures

Your answers become the custom call-handling instructions we build into this account's AI Receptionist — the CAP. Describe the routing, questions, and call endings you want AIR to follow. You're telling us what AIR should do, not documenting the whole account. No need to re-state what AIR already does on its own (it always takes the caller's name and number and asks "is there anything else?" before ending) or what's configured elsewhere (FAQs, company info, scheduling).
